About

John and Tammy Glover Have Been Building Here Since 2003.

Tidewater Custom Modular Homes was built on the idea that a custom home should mean exactly that. Not a factory package with limited choices. Not a tract builder's standard plan. A home designed around the family that is going to live in it.

John and Tammy have been building in Hampton Roads for over 20 years. They know the region, the soil, the codes, and what it takes to deliver a modular build that holds up and looks right. Every project gets their direct attention.
