About Pete Eaves

A Lifetime in Marine Surveying

Pete Eaves has been in the marine industry since 1980. As president of Eaves Marine Solutions, he has conducted surveys on sail and power vessels of all makes and sizes throughout Hampton Roads and the Chesapeake Bay since 2002.

His background spans structural and cosmetic repairs, mechanical systems, and marina management across both the Great Lakes and the East Coast. Accredited by the Society of Accredited Marine Surveyors and licensed by the US Coast Guard, Pete brings credentials and hands-on experience that few surveyors in the region can match.
