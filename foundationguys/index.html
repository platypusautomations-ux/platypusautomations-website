What We Do

Complete Foundation and Crawl Space Services

From encapsulation to helical piers, Greg and his crew handle every aspect of your home's foundation and moisture protection.

Crawl Space Encapsulation

Full vapor barrier installation that seals out moisture, improves air quality, and protects your home's structure from the ground up.

Foundation Repair

Cracked walls, settling slabs, bowing foundation walls. We diagnose the root cause and fix it correctly the first time.

Helical Pier Installation

Deep foundation support using helical piers to stabilize and lift structures that have settled or shifted over time.

French Drain Installation

Redirects groundwater and surface runoff away from your foundation before it can cause moisture damage.

Dehumidifier Installation

Commercial-grade crawl space dehumidifiers that maintain safe humidity levels year-round, protecting against mold and rot.

Moisture and Termite Damage Mitigation

Assessment and remediation of structural damage caused by moisture intrusion and wood-destroying insects.

Crawl Space Insulation

Removal of old, damaged, or contaminated insulation and replacement with clean, properly installed material.
