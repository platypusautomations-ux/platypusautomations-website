Our Story

From Texas to Olde Towne

Perrine Winery started as a family dream in College Station, Texas. Decades of hard work, more than a few award-winning bottles, and a peacock crest that became the family mark.

Holland Leopold, daughter of the founders, is bringing the name, the crest, and the wines to a new tasting room on High Street in Olde Towne Portsmouth, Virginia. Women-owned and disabled-owned. A family story that keeps going.

The peacock crest traveled with them. It means the same thing it always has.

The Perrine peacock crest. A family symbol, now in Virginia.
