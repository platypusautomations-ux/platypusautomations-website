Our Story

A Family Legacy in Every Bottle

Perrine Winery started as a family dream in 2011, when Beth and Rick set out to make wines worth celebrating. More than a decade and over 80 national and international competition medals later, the wines speak for themselves.

Now their daughter Holland is carrying the legacy forward, bringing Perrine's award-winning wines to a new home in Olde Towne Portsmouth, where a historic neighborhood and a gathering community are the perfect backdrop for what this family built.
