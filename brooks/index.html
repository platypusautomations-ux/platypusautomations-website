Who We Are

Nearly Five Decades of Craftsmanship

For nearly five decades, Brooks Landscaping has been the design-build partner that Hampton Roads homeowners trust to create outdoor spaces they never want to leave. Founded in 1976 and headquartered right here in Chesapeake, our team brings deep regional knowledge to every project - from intimate backyard retreats to expansive estate landscapes.

We are a complete, full-service company: we design it, build it, and stand behind it. Our work speaks for itself through referrals from neighbors, friends, and longtime clients who have watched their landscapes mature and thrive over the years.

Every property is unique. We approach each project with fresh eyes - listening first and designing second - because the best landscape is the one that fits how you actually live outdoors.
