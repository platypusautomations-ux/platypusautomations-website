Who We Are

Veteran-Owned. Family-Operated. Built on Reputation.

Brooks Tree Service has been a trusted name in Hampton Roads tree care since 1976. Charles Brooks, a military veteran, founded the company and built it on a simple promise: show up when you say you will, do the work right, and leave the property cleaner than you found it.

Today, Charles's son Ryan leads crews on every job. With an ISA Certified Arborist on staff and decades of experience removing everything from crowded backyard pines to storm-damaged oaks hanging over rooftops, the Brooks crew handles the work other companies turn down.

Spotless cleanup is not a bonus with us - it is part of the job. Every chip hauled, every log cut and stacked or removed. Our neighbors in Chesapeake and across Hampton Roads keep calling us back because they know the yard will look better when we leave than when we arrived.

What We Do

Complete Tree Care for Hampton Roads

From a single stump to a full-property canopy assessment, we handle every tree job with the same care and attention to cleanup.

Tree Removal

Safe, controlled removal of any tree - tight spaces, large canopies, close proximity to structures. Full cleanup included every time.

Tree Trimming and Pruning

Proper pruning for structure, clearance, and long-term health. We cut for the tree, not just for looks - and it shows over time.

Stump Grinding

Grinding stumps below grade so they do not sprout, trip anyone, or ruin your lawn. Clean fill hauled or spread on site, your choice.

Storm Damage and Emergency Response

24/7 response for downed trees, split limbs on structures, and hazardous post-storm situations. We pick up before insurers arrive if needed.

Canopy Reduction

Reducing height and spread to manage clearance from rooflines, power lines, and neighboring properties - without sacrificing the tree.

Firewood

Seasoned split firewood from removed trees, available for delivery or pickup. Hardwood mix, stacked neatly, ready to burn.
