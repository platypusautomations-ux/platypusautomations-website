Common Questions

Website & GBP FAQ

How long does it take to build a website?
We can build it in 2-3 days, but we typically allow 1-2 weeks. Why? Because rushing undercuts the value. A professional site needs thought on copy, photos, and positioning. We take the time to get it right so you launch with confidence, not speed.
Do I own the website?
You have two options. Option 1: You buy it outright. You own the domain, content, and hosting. You can take it over anytime or move it elsewhere. Option 2: You rent it from us. We host, maintain, and manage it for a monthly fee. Either way works — it depends on whether you want to own it or prefer us to handle everything.
Will my website rank on Google?
Yes — it's built for local SEO. We handle technical optimization (speed, mobile, structure), content optimization (business type, service area), and Google Business Profile optimization so you rank for searches in your area. Most clients see meaningful improvements within 60 days.
Can I update the website myself?
No. We manage updates for you. When you need to change text, photos, hours, or services, you send us the info and we make the updates. This keeps the site clean, consistent, and secure. Updates are available as part of a hosting package or as individual requests.
What if I already have a website?
We audit it. If it's solid, we optimize it (SEO, Google Business Profile, add contact forms). If it's outdated or not converting, we recommend rebuilding. Either way, the outcome is the same: you rank on Google and convert searchers into customers.
Is this required before I use your other services?
Not technically — but practically, yes. Without a website and claimed Google Business Profile, you're not generating leads to automate. It's the prerequisite. The other services (missed call text-back, AI screening, reviews) all layer on top of this foundation.
