What We Do

Complete Tree Care for Hampton Roads

From a single problem tree to a full property clearout, Dave's Cove handles every job with the same attention to detail — and the same commitment to cleanup.

Tree Removal

Safe, controlled removal of any tree — tight yards, close to structures, large canopies. Full cleanup included on every job, no exceptions.

Tree Trimming & Pruning

Proper pruning for structure, clearance, and long-term health. We cut for the tree, not just for looks — and the results show over time.

Stump Grinding

Grinding stumps below grade so they don't sprout, trip anyone, or ruin your lawn. Clean fill hauled or spread on site — your call.

Storm Damage & Emergency Response

Downed trees, split limbs on structures, hazardous post-storm situations. David answers the phone — call any time when it's urgent.

Land & Lot Clearing

Full lot clearouts for construction prep, fence lines, or reclaiming overgrown property. Trees, brush, and debris hauled clean.

Canopy Reduction

Reducing height and spread to manage clearance from rooflines, power lines, and neighboring properties — without sacrificing the tree.
